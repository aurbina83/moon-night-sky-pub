22 Until None has contacts to local resources, and be there 24/7 to talk to any veteran in need to talk to someone. The staff at 22 Until None are all veterans from several different eras and we have both male and female staff to assist. You had our back, now it's time we have yours.

Battle in Distress

Battle In Distress is committed to reducing the 23 Warfighter suicides a day. We commit to preventing the stigmas that lead Warfighters to take their lives and address the issues that take them down that path. We will do this through education, peer support, and intervention.

Veteran Crisis Hotline

The Veterans Crisis Line connects Veterans in crisis and their families and friends with qualified, caring Department of Veterans Affairs responders through a confidential toll-free hotline, online chat, or text.
